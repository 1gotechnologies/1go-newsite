About travas

travas

Explore is a tour aggregator platform allowing tourist from all over the world to book tours and activities with amazing tour operators in Africa. We help you discover the rich and hidden beauty of Africa an experience that is sure to be a lifelong memory for you. Search, compare and select amazing deals on tour to Africa. For even more transparency, we let you view your tour guide even before booking.

Request tour:
Request a tour based on your preferences such as destination, date, budget etc.

Easy and flexible booking:
Amazing memory-rich experiences are just a few clicks away. Enjoy hassle-free booking process and secure payment method. Change your mind about travelling? Well feel free to cancel your booking. Transparent booking At explore we are big on transparency and security during your booking process. Get interesting info about your tour guide even before completing your booking process.

24/7 support:
Having issues booking a tour? Well worry not, cause from the start to the end of your booking process, we would be there for you.

Whether you are an individual or a multi-national operator, explore is there for you. We help you scale up your business by helping you showcase your amazing products/packages to a worldwide audience.
